Welome to Ellawala & Das Holdings (Pvt) Ltd.

Ellawala & Das Holdings (Pvt) Ltd is one of the promising companies which specializes in doing humanitarian projects for the developments and uplifting of a country keeping a close supervision on the needs of her people and nature.

Pertaining to the fact that the Company is standing on "Three Solid Pilars - Nature, Technology and Innovation", our vision is to keep a balance and harmony between nature and innovation without disturbing the ecology by any means. With cutting edge technological advancements we aim to deliver the best technology as per requirement so that the nature is not compromised.

Our mission is to bring innovation ‘with the nature’ and ‘for the nature’. Sri Lanka is a beautiful island country with a diverse flora and fauna. We aim to preserve our natural & cultural heritage, our wildlife and our people.

We also bring investments to the Governments on humaniterian grounds to facilitate the needs of the developing countries to initiate more and more development projects.
